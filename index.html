Known Issues & Troubleshooting

  • Can’t log in: Make sure you’re using the correct email registered for access. If the issue persists, contact support with your device model and iOS version.
  • Content not loading: Check your internet connection and try again. If the problem continues, restart the app.
  • Forgot password reset: If you don’t receive the reset email, check your spam folder. It may also be easier to complete the reset from a computer rather than a phone.
  • App feedback or feature requests: Send details to the support email above.
